PIZZA SALES DATA ANALYSIS.

ABOUT.

This project delves into analyzing pizza sales data to understand customer preferences, popular pizzas, and sales trends. By exploring factors like size, category, price, and ingredients, we aim to help pizza businesses optimize their menus, target marketing efforts, and make informed decisions about pricing and inventory.
